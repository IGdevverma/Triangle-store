Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Return Eligibility

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized Team Kits
  • Bulk Manufacturing Orders
  • Products damaged due to misuse

3. Refund Process

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

4. Exchange

Size exchanges are available subject to stock availability.

5. Damaged Products

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
