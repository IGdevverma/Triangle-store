About Triangle Sports

IT ALL STARTED WITH A VISION

Welcome to Triangle Sports – Empowering Athletes, Refining Performance. Founded in 2016 , Triangle Sports began its journey with a simple vision: to deliver high-quality, durable, and comfortable sportswear to athletes and fitness enthusiasts. We started as a passionate trading enterprise, deeply understanding the evolving needs of the sports apparel market and the demands of modern athletes.

Our Evolution: From Vision to Manufacturing Excellence The year 2021 marked a defining milestone for us. Emerging stronger after the global pandemic, we transitioned from trading to setting up our very own state-of-the-art manufacturing unit. What began as a humble setup with just 5 to 6 stitching machines has today transformed into a robust, high-capacity production facility operating with approximately 100 advanced machines. This rapid growth is a testament to our unwavering commitment to premium quality, precision craftsmanship, and customer trust.

Why Choose Triangle Sports? In-House Manufacturing: Every piece of sportswear is crafted under our strict supervision, ensuring top-tier fabric quality and flawless stitching. Designed for Performance: Our apparel blends cutting-edge athletic designs with maximum comfort, flexibility, and durability. Scale & Reliability: With our expanded capacity of 100+ machines, we are fully equipped to handle high-volume demands efficiently without compromising on quality.

Our Commitment At Triangle Sports, we believe that the right gear can elevate your game. Whether you are a professional athlete, a sports team, or a fitness enthusiast, we are dedicated to providing you with sportswear that inspires confidence and boosts performance.
