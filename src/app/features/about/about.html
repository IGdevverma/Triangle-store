About Triangle Sports

IT ALL STARTED WITH A VISION

Triangle Sports was founded in 2021 by Mrs. Shobha Verma with a simple yet powerful vision — to create premium sportswear that inspires confidence, enhances performance, and helps teams perform at their very best.

What began as a dream in 2021 to redefine custom sportswear manufacturing has today evolved into a trusted partner for institutions, brands and organizations across India.

From custom team jerseys to private-label manufacturing, every product reflects our commitment to quality, innovation and customer satisfaction.
