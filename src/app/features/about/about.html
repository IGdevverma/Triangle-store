About Triangle Sports

Driven by Quality. Built for Performance.

India's Trusted Sportswear Manufacturing Brand

Triangle Sports is a premium sportswear brand proudly owned and manufactured by Trian Industries Pvt. Ltd., established in 2021 with a vision to redefine the Indian sportswear industry through innovation, quality, and performance.

At Trian Industries Pvt. Ltd., every product is manufactured using carefully selected fabrics, advanced production techniques, and strict quality control standards. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every garment delivers superior comfort, durability, flexibility, and long-lasting performance.

Triangle Sports is designed for athletes, fitness enthusiasts, professionals, and individuals who believe that high-quality sportswear should enhance both performance and confidence. From training apparel and gym wear to active lifestyle clothing, our products are created to meet the demands of modern sports and fitness.

We continuously invest in research, innovation, and manufacturing excellence to create products that combine functionality with modern style. Every stitch reflects our dedication to craftsmanship, ensuring our customers receive sportswear they can trust every day.

As we continue to grow, our mission remains simple—to become one of India's most trusted sportswear manufacturers by delivering premium quality products, exceptional customer satisfaction, and innovative designs that inspire people to perform their best.
