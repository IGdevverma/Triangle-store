Our Story

Founded in 2016, Triangle Sports began its journey with a simple vision—to deliver high-quality, durable, and comfortable sportswear for athletes and fitness enthusiasts. We started as a passionate trading enterprise, gaining valuable insight into the evolving sports apparel industry and the performance needs of modern athletes.

Our Evolution

The year 2021 marked a defining milestone in our journey. After the global pandemic, we expanded our vision by establishing our own state-of-the-art manufacturing unit. What began with just 5–6 stitching machines has now grown into a modern production facility equipped with 100+ advanced machines.

This remarkable growth reflects our unwavering commitment to premium quality, precision craftsmanship, innovation, and the trust our customers place in us.
